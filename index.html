FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • DO I NEED TO BRING A CAMERA?

    No - the camera is built-in. The studio is fully rigged up and ready to shoot. You will be able to control camera height and other parameters using the controls.

  • WILL SOMEONE BE THERE TO HELP WITH ANY QUESTIONS?

    Yes - our staff will check you in, show you the studio controls, then let you shoot privately. You'll be able to step outside the studio area to ask questions any time.

  • WHAT IS THE PARKING SITUATION?

    There is plenty of street parking (2-hour and longer) in the neighborhood. You'll get all our detailed parking guidance upon booking.

  • HOW DO I GET MY PHOTOS AFTER I'M DONE?

    We'll email you a password-protected Dropbox link with all your photos on the same day. No need to install any app - you'll be able to view and download your photos on most any mobile/desktop internet browser, whether on iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, or otherwise.

  • CAN I TAKE FULL-BODY SHOTS?

    Unfortunately full-body shots are not possible. Our studio is best for headshots up to 3/4-length shots.

  • WHAT ABOUT PHOTO RETOUCHING?

    All photos are automatically processed/filtered, but we offer a manual retouching service for stray hairs, eye brightening, skin smoothening etc. starting at $15/photo.

  • DO YOU OFFER A HAIR & MAKEUP SERVICE?

    Not at the moment, but you may bring an HMU artist with you if you like.
